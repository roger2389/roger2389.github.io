About Me

Hello! I'm Roger, a dedicated quantitative professional with a strong background honed through academic pursuits and diverse internship experiences. My passion lies at the intersection of finance, mathematics, and technology, driving me towards a career as a Quantitative Trader and Researcher.

Currently pursuing a Master's degree at National Chengchi University (NCCU), I am deepening my expertise in quantitative trading strategies and empirical asset allocation. My academic foundation was built during my Bachelor's studies at National Sun Yat-sen University.

Throughout my journey, I've had the privilege of interning at various reputable firms, including SQM Technologies, PSC, MBQ, EDGE Quantitative Trading, TEJ, and Crypto Arsenal. These experiences provided invaluable insights into real-world quantitative applications, from developing factor models and machine learning algorithms to optimizing portfolios and analyzing time-series data.

Beyond technical skills, I've actively contributed to the quantitative community, serving as a Team Researcher at the Jams Capital Portfolio Management Team and a Team Mentor at the TMBA Algorithm Trading Team. I am constantly seeking opportunities to learn, innovate, and contribute to the evolving field of quantitative finance.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to learn more about my skills and experiences, or reach out via the contact section.
